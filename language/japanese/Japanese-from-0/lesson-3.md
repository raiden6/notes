
# Lesson 3

## New words
- dare - who?
- watashi - I; me (male or female)
- boku - I; me (male)
- anata - you
- kanojo - her; she; girlfriend
- kare - him; he; boyfriend
- namae - name
- tomodachi - friend
- tabun - maybe
- gakkou - school
- tori - bird
- sensei - teacher
- nihongo no sensei - Japanese teacher

## New Phrases
- soudesu - That's right; it is; yes
- chigaimasu - That's wrong: its different; it isn't; no
